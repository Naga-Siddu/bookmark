PAWAN KALYAN

Konidela Pawan Kalyan is an Indian actor, filmmaker, politician and founder of the Jana Sena Party. He is currently serving as the Deputy Chief Minister of ANDHRA PRADESH

  • Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi.
  • His second film Gokulamlo Seeta released the following year.
  • Kalyan founded a political party named Jana Sena Party on 14 March 2014.



    • THALAPATHY VIJAY

    Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema.

  • Born in Madras to director S. A. Chandrasekhar, Vijay made his debut as a child actor in the Tamil film Vetri (1984).
  • In 2003, he starred in Ghilli, which became the first Tamil film to gross over ₹500 million and with subsequent success of Thirupaachi and Pokkiri later,
  • He was featured in Eastern Eye top celebrities in Asia, and he is the only Tamil actor to feature among Bollywood actors in the list in 2023

  • Bujjigadu (2008), Billa (2009), Darling (2010), Mr. Perfect (2011), and Mirchi (2013),
